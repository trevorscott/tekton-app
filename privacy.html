Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 25, 2026

Tekton is a personal performance app built by Trevor Scott. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. Tekton is designed with privacy as a default — your data is used only to generate guidance for you, and is never sold or shared for commercial purposes.

01 — Who we are

Tekton is an independently developed iOS application created by Trevor Scott. References to "we," "our," or "the app" in this policy refer to Tekton and its developer.

02 — Data we collect

Tekton collects the following categories of data, all of which you provide directly or authorize through connected services:

  • Biometric data from WHOOP: recovery score, heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate (RHR), sleep duration and stages, sleep performance, strain score, and workout activity data
  • Workout data you log directly in the app: exercises, sets, reps, and weight
  • Nutrition data you log directly in the app: meal names, calorie counts, and protein intake
  • Habit tracking data: habit names, descriptions, and daily completion records
  • Body weight entries you log in the app
  • Personal goals and preferences: calorie target, protein target, weekly workout frequency, and primary fitness goal

Tekton does not collect your name, email address, precise location, contacts, camera data, or any data unrelated to health and fitness.

03 — How your data is used

Your data is used for one purpose: generating personalized daily guidance through the Tekton AI engine. Specifically:

  • Your biometric, nutrition, workout, habit, and goal data is assembled into a structured context and sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a daily guidance recommendation
  • This guidance tells you what action to prioritize given the current state of your body and goals
  • Historical data is used to compute trends displayed within the app

Your data is never used for advertising, data mining, analytics profiling, or any purpose other than generating guidance for you personally.

04 — AI processing and Anthropic

To generate guidance, Tekton sends a structured summary of your health and fitness data to the Claude API, operated by Anthropic, PBC. This includes biometric signals, today's nutrition and habit state, recent workout history, and your goals.

This data is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection. Anthropic processes this data to generate a text response and is subject to their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Tekton does not include your name, email address, or any directly identifying information in requests sent to the Claude API. Requests contain only health and fitness metrics.

05 — WHOOP integration

If you connect your WHOOP account, Tekton accesses your WHOOP data through the official WHOOP Developer API using OAuth 2.0 authorization. You explicitly authorize this access during the connection flow.

  • Your WHOOP access and refresh tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device
  • Tekton accesses only the WHOOP data types necessary to generate guidance: recovery, sleep, cycles, workouts, and body measurements
  • You can disconnect your WHOOP account at any time from within the app, which immediately deletes all stored tokens
  • WHOOP data is also subject to WHOOP's own privacy policy

06 — Data storage and security

Your data is stored primarily on your device using iOS Core Data, a local encrypted database. Specifically:

  • All workout, nutrition, habit, weight, and goal data lives on your device only
  • WHOOP OAuth tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is hardware-backed encrypted storage
  • Tekton does not operate a cloud database or server that stores your personal health data
  • Data sent to the Claude API is transmitted over TLS-encrypted connections

07 — Data sharing

Tekton does not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal data with any third party except as described in this policy. The only external service that receives your data is Anthropic's Claude API, solely for the purpose of generating guidance responses, as described in Section 04.

Tekton does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

08 — Data retention and deletion

Because your data is stored on your device, you control it entirely. You can delete any data from within the app at any time. Deleting the app from your device removes all locally stored data.

Tekton does not retain copies of your data on external servers. Data sent to the Claude API for guidance generation is not persistently stored by Tekton after the response is received.

09 — Children's privacy

Tekton is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

10 — Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
